Graphic Art Art Direction Dev – November 2021

Visual novel
Team of 3
3 days production (+ 7 days conception)

Role during production: Developper, Art Director and Illustrator

In a post-apocalyptic world (toxic fungus), one faction is living safely under a dome while the other struggles to survive in a hostile environnement. The main character managed to integrate the dome society and is now conducting an criminal investigation, which leads him to reconnect with the outside world.


  • Society inside the dome: geared towards the past (greco-roman inspirations), hypocritical rejection of the technology that allows them to thrive.


  • Society outside the dome: adaptation (copper plates and powder used as antifungal), creativity and community as cornerstones
