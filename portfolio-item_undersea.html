Under the Sea

It is Now collaboration

Interactive experience in Unity

The project started as a short interactive expérience created during a workshop in a team of 8 for the "It is Now" NGO, which deploys art installations in healthcare infrastructures.
My teams' creation was selected to be displayed and there was a will to bring the project to a more complete and rich state.
For this reason, I am collaborating with the association as a generalist artist and technical artist to improve the experience.
I am reworking some of the visual elements, adding new interactive creatures and an system to make the scene composition and ambiance evolve in time.
