Moodseed is a connected object design school project made in a week with team of 5 students.

Moodseed is a terrarium that lights up when held and whose light pattern will differ depending on the holder and the plant's growth.

Along with participating in the design aspect, my role was to create this 3d model of the object, along with other visuals and the video montage of a product presentation.

The 3d model was made in Blender using geometry nodes.
