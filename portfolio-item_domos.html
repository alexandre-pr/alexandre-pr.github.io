In a post-apocalyptic world riddled by a toxic fungus, one faction is living safely under a dome while the other struggles to survive in a hostile environnement.

Domos is a visual novel made in Unity as part of a 1 week school project with a team of 3.

The main character Arthur managed to integrate the dome society and is now conducting an criminal investigation, which leads him to reconnect with the outside world.
