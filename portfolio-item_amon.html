Follow the young shepherd Amon on his quest to reunite his herd scattered around the lands of Astera.

Amon is a 3D puzzle adventure game were you play as Amon and collaborate with your sheep herd to solve various puzzle to overcome the obstacles on your way to reunite your herd and find a new home together.

The gameplay revolves around your bell that can be used to guide your sheeps and a magic rod given to you by one of Astera's god that can transform your sheeps in various creatures using the power of the ground beneath you.

The game offers an adventure on the theme of transhumance where you can explore, alongside your sheeps, colorful environments inspired by the Mediterranean's landscapes.
